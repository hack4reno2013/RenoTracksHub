Development

RenoTracks is a joint project created during the 2013 Hack 4 Reno hackathon in order to develop a reliable and accurate method of collecting cycling data from Reno bicyclists.

RenoTracks is based on the CycleTracks app originally developed for the San Francisco Country Transportation Authority and the Cycle Atlanta app.

RenoTracks is an app for recording your bicycle trips. Using the app helps the City of Reno better understand bike routes and improve transportation efficacy throughout the city and Washoe County.

By using your phone's GPS to record your routes, city planners will get to know which routes cyclists prefer. And if you see a problem on the road, like potholes or obstructed bike lanes, RenoTracks will allows you to contact the right authorities to get it fixed.
